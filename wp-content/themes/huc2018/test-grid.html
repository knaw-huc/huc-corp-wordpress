Study

HuC is constantly searching for ambitious interns and final-year students. We are looking for students who are fascinated to get a taste of one of the world’s most prominent knowledge companies. Your internship or graduation project at HuC? Would you like to get to know HuC during your studies? Why not do your internship or graduation project at our organisation? Around 400 students do each year. It’s a great way of starting your career within HuC.

Starters / PhD

As a starter at HuC you have many options. You can specialize or you can develop in a broader sense. You could become a boardroom consultant or top researcher. Or maybe you see yourself as an R&D engineer or leading a project. HuC offers you fantastic prospects in which you have a great deal of freedom and responsibility in shaping your career.Currently, we are mainly looking for students and graduates in the disciplines: Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Mathematics, Econometrics, Electrical Engineering, Physics, Aerospace Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. And also for other educational backgrounds, we always have challenging jobs, internships and graduation assignments.HuC presents you with a wealth of options. What is your aim: to become a top researcher or a consultant, project manager or business developer? From the moment you enter employment with us you enjoy major freedom in, and responsibility for, shaping your own career path.PhD studies and positions at HuC are closely linked with our research initiatives and departments. The doctoral education programme combines academics with methodological schooling and hands on experience.
